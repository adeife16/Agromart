About Farmart

The Farmart Market is an events center ofering the charm of a vintage barn with the accommodations of a fresh built space. Featuring Bride & Groom quarters, restrooms, and a warming kitchen for outside caterers, we are prepared for all of your event needs.

We are breaking ground on The Farmart Market in May 2020 and are anticipating a Grand Opening in Fall 2020. The Farmhouse Barn land is available for weddings and events before the Grand Opening.
